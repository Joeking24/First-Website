About Me

I have always been interested in how computers work. Whether it be the actual hardware of the computer itself, programs that I use frequently, video games, or webpages. My growing interest in these types of topics has pushed me to follow this path towards a potential career.

So far, each new thing I have learned has created many more questions that spawn their own ideas as well. The learning process seems unending as technology continues to evolve. Having the chance to take part in this process is already filling me with joy and I hope to continue to do so!

I am very excited to start sharing my passion for web development and software engineering with others who might feel the same way. In my opinion, sharing ideas with other people is how even greater ideas are formed. I hope to be able to start to build a network of individuals who are interested in these topics, as that will help all of us in the long run!

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, cooking new and exciting foods, perfecting my existing recipes, watching anime, and listening to all different kinds of music!
